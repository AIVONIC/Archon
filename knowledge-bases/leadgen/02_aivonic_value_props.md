## What Makes Aivonic Different (Use These in Emails)

### 1. We Build It For You
- Not a DIY chatbot platform (unlike Intercom, Drift, Zendesk)
- Not expensive custom development ($100K-500K agencies)
- Total client time investment: ~1 hour (discovery call + onboarding form)
- Agent is production-ready same day

### 2. Voice-Enabled Conversations
- Customers can literally talk to your AI agent — no typing needed
- Premium voice quality (Hume AI, ElevenLabs)
- Emotionally intelligent responses
- Particularly valuable for mobile users and accessibility
- No competitor in our price range offers this

### 3. Infinite Memory
- Agent remembers every customer interaction forever
- 50M+ message capacity
- Customers never have to repeat themselves
- Standard chatbots forget after ~20 messages or when session ends
- Enables truly personalized customer experience over time

### 4. 29 Languages, Zero Configuration
- Automatic language detection — customer writes in Spanish, agent responds in Spanish
- Nordic: Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Finnish, Icelandic
- Western European: English, German, French, Spanish, Dutch, Portuguese, Italian
- Central/Eastern European: Polish, Czech, Romanian, Hungarian, Bulgarian, Croatian, Slovak, Slovenian, Serbian, Ukrainian
- Baltic: Lithuanian, Latvian, Estonian
- Other: Greek, Turkish, Arabic
- No per-language setup or extra fees

### 5. Certified Quality
- 4-phase pre-deployment evaluation (infrastructure, functional, quality, security)
- Every conversation automatically scored by AI quality system
- Quality badges: Gold (8+/10), Silver (6-7.9), Bronze (4-5.9)
- Agents get better automatically over time (self-improvement)
- No bad agents ever reach customers

### 6. Agents That DO Things
- Not just answering questions — executing real tasks
- Send emails, search the web, browse websites, book appointments
- Human escalation with full conversation context
- 31 built-in skills across 6 categories

- "29 languages" with zero configuration
- "50M+ messages" memory capacity

- "24/7" availability

## What NOT to Say in Cold Emails

- Don't say "proprietary technology" or "patent-pending" (sounds like hype)
- Don't mention compression ratios (too technical)
- Don't lead with pricing in the first email
- Don't claim specific ROI numbers unless backed by real case data
- Don't compare directly to named competitors (let them ask)
