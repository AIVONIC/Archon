# AI Consulting for Construction and Real Estate Firms

E3Vantage deploys AI agent technology for construction and real estate firms who want to modernize their client engagement and back-office operations.

## Client-Facing AI Agents

We deploy AI agents that handle the front line of client engagement for firms:

- 24/7 availability in 29 languages
- Intelligent property matching from natural language queries
- Automated lead qualification and scoring
- Persistent memory across conversations (returning clients get continuity, not a cold restart)
- Smart escalation to human agents when needed
- Embeddable chat widget for any website
- Voice input and output support

The agent is branded to the firm. Their logo, their colors, their tone. Clients interact with what feels like the firm's own assistant, not a third-party tool.

## Back-Office Automation (Co-Pilot)

For firms that want internal efficiency, we deploy back-office automation:

- Lead processing: automatic ingestion, scoring, and organization from web forms, landing pages, and social media
- CMA automation: the agent calculates comparative market analysis from comp data the agent provides, generating professional branded PDF reports in under 60 seconds
- Newsletter automation: weekly market newsletters scraped from public sources, branded, and delivered to the firm's email list

The co-pilot runs in the background. Event-driven, no manual prompting needed for routine tasks.

## The Technology

Our agents are built on the Aivonic platform:

- NEXUS agent factory creates production-ready agents from natural language descriptions
- SAGE earned autonomy protocol governs how much independence each agent has
- Archon knowledge engine provides semantic search over property data, FAQs, and market intelligence
- Infinite Memory stores every conversation permanently
- Industry-specific guardrails prevent the agent from making claims about valuations, mortgage rates, future appreciation, or other legally sensitive topics

Every agent passes a 4-phase pre-deployment evaluation: infrastructure verification, functional testing (15+ scenarios), quality scoring by an independent AI judge, and security testing for prompt injection resistance.

## Quality Assurance

System prompts are not hand-written. They are engineered and scored by two AI sub-agents:

- AXIOM creates the system prompt (specialist prompt engineer)
- CRITIC scores it on 8 dimensions and rejects anything below 8.0/10

Dimensions include identity specificity, personality calibration, skill grounding, anti-patterns, escalation clarity, response architecture, guardrails, and opening behavior.
