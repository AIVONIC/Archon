# E3Vantage Team

## Lukas Angenendt - Architecture and Assessment

MA Architecture from MSA Munster. Published researcher on biomimetic spatial structures. Former MSF/Doctors Without Borders team member. University lecturer. Speaks German (native), English (C2), French (B2), Spanish (B2), and Dutch (B1). Over 15 years of international project experience across Australia, Germany, Spain, the United Kingdom, and Portugal. Based in Burgau, Western Algarve.

Relevant Algarve projects include the Laboratory for Natural Medicine in Vila do Bispo (off-grid, nature reserve), Casa Acosta (two apartments in Vila do Bispo), Ruin RC186 (historic ruin reactivation in Aljezur, fully off-grid), Casa Pedro (2,800 square meter feasibility study in Raposeira), and MobileHome (design and construction in Espiche), among others.

What Lukas does that AI cannot: on-site structural assessment, Portuguese municipal navigation (camaras and licensing), renovation feasibility evaluation, accurate cost estimation, contractor coordination, negotiation with local authorities, and relationship building with property owners and partners.

Lukas provides architectural judgment and local ground truth. Christian provides the AI platform and technology. Henrik provides cultural access and community connections.
