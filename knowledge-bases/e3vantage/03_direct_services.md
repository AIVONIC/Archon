# Direct Property and Construction Services

E3Vantage offers end-to-end property and construction services in the Western Algarve.

## Property Assessment and Feasibility Studies

Our architect conducts on-site assessments of properties to evaluate structural condition, renovation potential, planning constraints, and development feasibility. Each assessment produces a professional report covering:

- Structural condition and integrity
- Renovation scope and complexity
- Municipal zoning and planning status
- Estimated renovation budget ranges (indicative, not guaranteed)
- Development potential and use case recommendations
- Access, utilities, and infrastructure considerations

This service is particularly valuable for properties that are shells, ruins, or in need of significant renovation, where the gap between asking price and completed value is where the opportunity lives.

## Construction Project Management

From feasibility through completion, we manage the full construction process:

- Design development (working with our architect and local partners)
- Licensing and municipal approvals (camara processes)
- Contractor selection and coordination
- Budget management and progress tracking
- Quality assurance and site supervision
- Final licensing (Licenca de Utilizacao)

Our architect has direct experience navigating Portuguese municipal processes in Vila do Bispo, Lagos, and Aljezur.

## Visual Production

Every property we represent receives a complete marketing suite produced in-house:

- Professional photography (interior and exterior)
- Drone filming (licensed UAV operation)
- 3D rendering (Rhino 3D, SketchUp, Vray)
- Digital staging (showing renovation potential visually)
- Video walkthroughs and property presentations

This visual production capability means properties are presented at a standard most agencies cannot match, particularly for shell or renovation properties where showing the vision is critical to attracting the right buyers.

## Market Intelligence

We monitor the Western Algarve property market continuously:

- Price trends by municipality and property type
- Inventory levels and days on market
- International buyer demographics and demand patterns
- Planning and regulatory changes
- New development and infrastructure projects

This intelligence informs our assessments, pricing recommendations, and client advisory work.

## Property Sales Support

For property owners, we provide full-service sales support:

- AI-powered 24/7 multilingual buyer engagement
- Lead qualification and scoring
- Viewing scheduling and management
- Market-informed pricing guidance
- Visual production and marketing materials
- Transaction support through to completion

Our AI agent handles initial buyer engagement, qualification, and scheduling. Our architect handles assessments and viewings. Our construction capability means we can advise on and deliver renovations that maximize sale value.
