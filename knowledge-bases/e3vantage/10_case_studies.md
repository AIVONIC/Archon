# Case Studies and Project Portfolio

## Reference Case: Burgau Development Property

Our first autonomous AI property sales deployment. A 5,000 square meter three-level concrete shell in Burgau, 400 meters from the beach. Asking price EUR 600,000, which represents EUR 120 per square meter against an Algarve average of over EUR 3,200 per square meter for finished property.

The AI agent (algarve.forsale) handles the complete sales process:
- 24/7 engagement in English, Portuguese, German, and French
- Deep knowledge of the property, the market, and the Portuguese buying process
- Buyer qualification and profiling
- Investment scenario modeling
- Transaction guidance through all stages
- Email follow-up and lead nurturing

This deployment demonstrates the full E3Vantage technology stack applied to a single difficult property: a concrete shell that requires vision selling, not standard listing presentation.
