# Frequently Asked Questions

## About E3Vantage

Q: What is E3Vantage?
A: A technology-driven property consultancy in the Western Algarve, Portugal. We combine architectural expertise, construction capability, visual production, and AI-powered client engagement to serve property owners, real estate firms, and investors.

Q: Where do you operate?
A: Western Algarve, Portugal. Our core area runs from Lagos to Sagres, with particular depth in Burgau, Vila do Bispo, and the Lagos corridor.

Q: What languages do you support?
A: English, Portuguese, German, French, and Spanish. Our AI agent supports 29 languages for client engagement.

Q: Is E3Vantage a real estate agency?
A: We are a property consultancy with architectural and construction capabilities. We are investigating the AMI license for full brokerage activity in Portugal. We work alongside licensed agencies when required.

## For Property Owners

Q: How much does it cost to list my property with you?
A: Our fee structure depends on the service scope. For the difficult objects program, there is no upfront cost. We earn commission on successful sale. For full-service engagements, we discuss pricing during the assessment phase.

Q: What kind of properties do you work with?
A: We specialize in properties with development potential: shells, ruins, renovation projects, and land with building permission. We also work with completed properties, particularly those that have been on the market for an extended period.

Q: How long does the assessment take?
A: The on-site assessment typically takes half a day. The written report follows within a few business days, depending on complexity.

Q: Can you handle the construction as well?
A: Yes. Our architect manages construction projects from design through completion, including licensing, contractor coordination, and quality assurance.

## For Firms

Q: What does the AI agent actually do?
A: It handles client engagement on your website 24/7, in 29 languages. It answers property questions from your knowledge base, qualifies leads, schedules viewings, and escalates to your human agents when needed. It remembers every conversation and follows up with interested clients.

Q: Will it replace our agents?
A: No. It handles the initial engagement and qualification work that consumes your agents' time. Your agents get pre-qualified, scored leads with full conversation history, ready for human interaction where it matters most.

Q: How long does setup take?
A: A basic agent with your branding, knowledge base, and core workflows can be deployed within days. More complex integrations with CRM systems and custom workflows take longer.

Q: What about data privacy?
A: Conversations are stored securely. Your data is not shared with other clients. The agent operates within strict guardrails and never shares client information across conversations.

## For Investors

Q: What returns can I expect?
A: We do not guarantee returns. Property investment carries risk. We provide data-driven analysis and scenarios based on market data, but actual outcomes depend on many factors including development choices, market conditions, and timing.

Q: Do you manage rental properties?
A: We focus on acquisition, development, and sale. We can recommend property management partners for ongoing rental operations.

Q: Can you help with financing?
A: We do not provide financial advice. We can connect you with Portuguese banking contacts and independent financial advisors who specialize in property finance for international buyers.
