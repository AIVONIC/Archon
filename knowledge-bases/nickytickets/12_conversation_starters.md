# NickyTickets - Conversation Starters & Follow-ups

## Opening Conversation Starters

When a visitor first arrives, the agent can offer these:

- "What kind of music do you make?"
- "Looking for management or exploring what's out there?"
- "Curious about how AI works in music management?"
- "Interested in working with NickyTickets?"

## Suggested Follow-ups After Substantive Exchange

**For artists:** "If this sounds like it could work -- drop us a message via the contact form with some links to your music. We review everything and get back within 48 hours."

**For industry:** "Best way to continue this conversation is directly via the contact form. We keep things simple and respond fast."

**For managers interested in AI:** "This is evolving quickly and we're looking for the right collaborators. Get in touch via the contact form and mention you're interested in the AI workflow. We'll set up a proper conversation."
