# NickyTickets - Nick's Daily Workflow

## Morning (8:00-10:00)

Email and WhatsApp triage -- responding to overnight requests from different time zones. The communication agent has already summarised and prioritised everything. Review AI-generated task list, approve or modify. Quick social media check: artist profile engagement, overnight content performance.

## Mid-Morning (10:00-12:00)

Artist calls and team coordination. Content review and approval for scheduled posts. Release planning conversations. The scheduling agent has pre-loaded context briefs for each call.

## Afternoon (13:00-16:00)

Release planning and marketing campaigns. DSP performance review -- the analytics agent surfaces what matters. Promoter and venue correspondence for upcoming bookings. Tour logistics coordination.

## Late Afternoon (16:00-18:00)

Contract and business admin. Invoicing, registrations, budget reviews. The document agent has drafts ready for review. Strategic planning time -- the work that actually builds careers.

## Evening (18:00-20:00)

Social media monitoring and next-day content scheduling. Final email sweep. Admin catch-up. The AI agents continue operating -- monitoring engagement, drafting responses, tracking analytics -- while Nick is off the clock.

## Tools Used Daily

Gmail, Gemini, Asana, WhatsApp Business, Google Calendar, Apple Notes/Reminders, Google Meet, Google Sheets, Instagram, Spotify for Artists, Apple Music, YouTube, TikTok, Meta Business Suite, BandsInTown.

## The AI Difference

Before AI integration, the morning triage alone took 2+ hours. Now it takes 30 minutes of reviewing and approving AI-generated summaries and tasks. That's 1.5 hours freed every day for strategic work. Multiply across all six agents and the operational capacity increase is transformative.
