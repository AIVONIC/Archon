# NickyTickets - Platform Knowledge

## Spotify for Artists

Real-time streaming data: streams, saves, listeners, followers. Audience demographics: age, gender, location, device. Audience segments: newly discovered, light, moderate, super listeners. Playlist tracking: which playlists feature the artist, stream contribution per playlist. Editorial playlist pitching: submit unreleased tracks at least 7 days before release. Campaign Kit: Marquee (paid promotion), Discovery Mode (algorithmic boost), Showcase. Canvas: looping visuals on tracks. Clips: short videos for fan connection. Concert listing and merch integration.

Key metrics: save-to-stream ratio (industry average 2-3%, above 5% = strong), listener-to-follower conversion, playlist discovery vs organic, geographic listening patterns (tour routing), Release Radar performance.

Playlist pitching best practices: deliver to distributor 4+ weeks before release, pitch 7+ days before, be specific about genre/mood, include marketing plans and press, pitch consistently.

## Apple Music for Artists

Streaming data, Shazam integration (real-world discovery indicator), radio play data, geographic insights. Less detailed than Spotify but essential for understanding the non-Spotify audience.

## YouTube / YouTube Music

Third-largest streaming platform. YouTube Studio: video performance, audience retention, traffic sources, revenue. Music videos and audio on same platform. Critical for visual content strategy.

## TikTok

Primary new music discovery platform in 2026. Short-form video drives streaming spikes. Analytics: video performance, sound usage by other creators, audience demographics. Difficult to engineer but amplifiable when organic momentum appears.

## Instagram / Meta Business Suite

Core artist branding platform. Stories, Reels, Feed serve different functions. Meta Business Suite for posting, analytics, advertising across Instagram and Facebook. Third-party ad approval management.

## BandsInTown / SongKick

Tour date listing platforms synced with Spotify and artist websites. Fan discovery of upcoming shows. Manager ensures dates are accurate and coordinated across all platforms.

## Asana

Project management hub. Tasks created from email/WhatsApp action items. Assigned with deadlines, tracked through completion. Integration with the communication triage agent.

## Booking Platforms: ABOSS / System One

Calendar management and booking coordination for live performances. Maintaining multiple client calendars and coordinating with agents and promoters.

## Distribution

DistroKid, TuneCore, CD Baby, Believe, Vydia, AWAL. If signed, label handles distribution; manager oversees process and ensures marketing requirements are met.

## Collection Societies

PRS (UK performing rights), PPL (UK neighbouring rights), Sound Exchange (US digital performance). Registration and management ensures the artist gets paid for all uses. Missing registrations = lost revenue.
