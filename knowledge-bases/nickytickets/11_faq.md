# NickyTickets - Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: What does NickyTickets actually do?**
A: Full-service AI-powered music management. We handle artist career development, international touring, marketing, release strategy, and business affairs -- with six specialised AI agents running the operational side so the manager can focus on strategy, taste, and relationships.

**Q: How is this different from a normal management company?**
A: Traditional management is limited by human bandwidth -- one person, maybe an assistant, doing everything manually across 15+ platforms. NickyTickets augments 12+ years of management experience with autonomous AI agents handling communication triage, social media, scheduling, analytics, tour logistics, and document management. The result: boutique personal attention with the operational capacity of a full team.

**Q: Are you replacing managers with AI?**
A: Exactly the opposite. We're making managers dramatically more effective. AI handles the 70% that's coordination and communication. The manager focuses on the 30% that actually builds careers -- creative direction, industry relationships, strategic decisions, and genuine care for the artist.

**Q: What genres do you work with?**
A: Nick's expertise and industry network are strongest in electronic music -- house, techno, melodic, deep, organic, and crossover styles. That said, the management principles and AI operational framework apply across all contemporary music. We're open to conversations with artists in adjacent genres.

**Q: What size artists do you work with?**
A: Our sweet spot is developing to established artists -- those with an existing body of work, some live performance history, and genuine ambition to build a sustainable career. We also selectively work with emerging talent that shows exceptional artistic identity and work ethic.

## For Artists

**Q: I'm an artist looking for management. How do I get started?**
A: Send us a message through the contact form or email. Tell us about your music, where you're at in your career, what you're looking for, and share some links to your work. We review every inquiry and respond within 48 hours.

**Q: What do you look for in an artist?**
A: A clear artistic identity (or the drive to develop one), a professional attitude, an existing body of work, realistic expectations paired with genuine ambition, and a collaborative mindset. Management is a partnership -- we both need to believe in where this is going.

**Q: How much does management cost?**
A: Every engagement is scoped individually based on the artist's needs, career stage, and the services required. The industry is moving toward commission structures that reflect the expanded value modern managers provide. We'll discuss specifics when we understand your situation.

**Q: Can I try working together before committing long-term?**
A: Absolutely. Our campaign management service is designed for exactly this -- a project-based engagement where we work together on a specific release, tour, or campaign. It's a chance for both sides to see if the partnership works before committing to full management.

**Q: What platforms do you manage?**
A: All major DSPs (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, TikTok, Amazon Music, Deezer), all major social platforms (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X), booking and tour platforms (BandsInTown, SongKick, ABOSS, System One), and project management (Asana, Google Workspace). If there's a platform relevant to your career, we either manage it or integrate with it.

## For Industry

**Q: I'm a promoter/label/brand. How do I work with NickyTickets?**
A: Get in touch via the contact form. Tell us what you're looking for -- whether it's booking one of our artists, exploring a collaboration, or discussing a brand partnership. We respond quickly and we value straightforward communication.

**Q: I'm a manager interested in your AI tools. Can I use them?**
A: We're actively looking for experienced managers to test and refine the AI workflow through real operations. This isn't a SaaS product yet -- it's a working system built by a working manager. If your experience could help shape the platform, we want to talk.

**Q: What technology powers the AI agents?**
A: Built on Aivonic's NEXUS platform. Multi-model AI architecture -- complex reasoning models for strategic tasks, fast models for operational processing. Voice interface via ElevenLabs and VAPI. Integrations with Gmail, WhatsApp Business API, Google Calendar, Asana, Spotify for Artists, YouTube, Meta Business Suite, TikTok, BandsInTown, and more. CRM and marketing automation through GoHighLevel. Everything runs with human approval gates -- no consequential actions without the manager's sign-off.

## About AI Integration

**Q: Is the AI actually working or is this just a concept?**
A: It's live. The agents are deployed and handling real management operations -- email triage, task creation, analytics, social scheduling. This isn't a pitch deck or a prototype. It's a working management operation with AI infrastructure running alongside a human manager every day.

**Q: Can the AI handle sensitive conversations with labels or promoters?**
A: The AI handles operational coordination -- scheduling, information gathering, routine updates. All relationship-critical conversations, negotiations, and sensitive discussions are handled by Nick personally. The AI frees up time for exactly those conversations.

**Q: What happens if the AI makes a mistake?**
A: Every agent operates under the earned autonomy model. They start observe-only: analyse, recommend, draft. Nothing goes out without manager approval until the agent proves reliable. Even at full autonomy, the manager retains override on everything. The worst case is a draft email that gets edited before sending -- not an action taken without permission.

**Q: How do you protect artist data?**
A: Role-based access controls, human approval gates on all consequential actions, encrypted data handling, and strict no cross-client data sharing. Your information stays in your workspace. We don't train AI models on your data or share it across our roster.
