# Aivonic - Client Portal

## Overview

Every Aivonic client gets access to a self-service dashboard at agents.aivonic.ai/dashboard. The portal gives full control over your agent, analytics, knowledge base, and billing without needing to contact support.

## Dashboard

The main dashboard shows an overview of agent activity including total conversations, active users, average response time, and quality score. A quality badge (Standard, Premium, or Elite) is prominently displayed so you always know how your agent is performing.

Quick actions let you restart your agent, view embed code, or upload documents with one click. Recent activity lists the latest conversations and any issues flagged by the monitoring system.

## Conversations

Full searchable history of every chat your agent has had with customers. You can filter by date, quality score, or search for specific keywords. Every conversation is automatically scored for quality. You can export conversations as CSV or JSON for your own analysis.

## Analytics

Real-time metrics you can filter by last 7, 30, or 90 days. Charts show conversation volume trends, peak hours, quality score distribution, and response times. Usage pattern analysis reveals when customers are most active, what devices they use, and typical conversation duration. Quality monitoring shows per-criteria breakdowns so you can see exactly where your agent excels and where it could improve.

## Knowledge Base Management

Drag-and-drop document upload supporting PDF, TXT, DOCX, and MD files up to 10MB each. Documents are automatically processed, chunked, and embedded for semantic search. You can test search queries directly in the portal to see what your agent would find. Stats cards show total documents, chunk counts, and storage usage. You can delete outdated materials or re-index the entire knowledge base at any time.

## Settings

Agent settings let you customize name, greeting message, conversational tone, and brand colors. API key management allows secure rotation with validity testing before saving. Notification preferences control email alerts for errors, weekly reports, and quality achievements. Advanced settings cover response timeouts, rate limiting, and auto-responses during downtime.

## Voice Settings

Toggle voice features on or off from the dashboard. Choose between three voice providers: ElevenLabs for high-quality multilingual voices, Hume AI for emotionally expressive voices, or OpenAI TTS for reliable standard quality. Each provider needs an API key you provide. A voice preview button lets you hear how each voice sounds before going live.

## Billing

Clear breakdown of your current usage including conversation counts, knowledge base storage, and bandwidth. Your support plan (Standard or Premium) and payment history are always visible. Invoices are downloadable as PDFs.

## Embed Code

One-click copy of the script tag you paste into your website. Works on WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Squarespace, and any platform that supports JavaScript. The widget is customizable with your brand colors, logo, and preferred theme (dark or light).
