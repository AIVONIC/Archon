# Aivonic - Industry Use Cases

## SaaS Companies

**Pain points:**
- High volume of repetitive support tickets (password resets, billing questions, feature how-tos)
- Support teams overwhelmed during launches and updates
- Customers churning because they cannot get quick answers
- After-hours coverage gaps

**How Aivonic helps:**
- AI agent handles tier-1 support instantly, 24/7
- Infinite memory means returning users never repeat their issue
- Escalation to human agents when needed, with full context preserved
- Knowledge base integration pulls answers directly from documentation
- Multilingual support for global user bases

## Digital Agencies and Consultancies

**Pain points:**
- Spending billable hours on initial client inquiries and qualification
- Inconsistent response times to prospect inquiries
- No coverage outside business hours
- Manual intake processes that delay engagement

**How Aivonic helps:**
- AI agent qualifies leads on the agency website 24/7
- Collects project requirements and budget information automatically
- Books discovery calls directly through appointment scheduling
- Hands off qualified leads to the team with full conversation context

## Real Estate

**Pain points:**
- Prospects want property information at all hours
- Agents spend time answering the same questions about listings
- Slow response to web inquiries means lost leads
- Managing inquiries across multiple listings

**How Aivonic helps:**
- AI agent answers property questions instantly from the knowledge base
- Schedules viewings through appointment booking integration
- Captures lead details and qualification criteria
- Voice-enabled conversations for mobile-first property searchers
- Multilingual support for international buyers

## E-Commerce

**Pain points:**
- Cart abandonment from unanswered product questions
- Order status inquiries flooding support channels
- Returns and exchange requests consuming agent time
- Seasonal spikes overwhelming support capacity

**How Aivonic helps:**
- Product recommendations and questions answered in real time
- Order tracking integration gives customers instant status updates
- Return processing handled through guided workflows
- Scales automatically during peak periods
- Remembers customer preferences and purchase history

## Healthcare Providers

**Pain points:**
- Phone lines tied up with appointment scheduling and rescheduling
- Patients asking repetitive questions about procedures, insurance, hours
- After-hours inquiries going unanswered until next business day
- Staff burnout from high-volume, low-complexity interactions

**How Aivonic helps:**
- AI agent handles appointment scheduling and reminders
- Answers common questions about services, insurance, and preparation
- Escalates clinical questions to human staff with proper context
- 24/7 availability for patient inquiries
- Strict guardrails prevent the agent from providing medical advice

## Legal Firms

**Pain points:**
- Potential clients inquiring outside business hours
- Time spent on initial consultations that do not convert
- Repetitive questions about practice areas, fees, and processes
- Client intake paperwork taking up attorney time

**How Aivonic helps:**
- AI agent qualifies potential clients based on practice area and case type
- Collects initial case details before the first consultation
- Answers questions about the firm's services and process
- Schedules consultations directly
- Strict guardrails ensure the agent never provides legal advice

## Hospitality and Restaurants

**Pain points:**
- Reservation inquiries at all hours
- Repetitive questions about menu, hours, location, dietary options
- Event and private dining inquiries needing quick responses
- Multilingual guests needing assistance

**How Aivonic helps:**
- AI agent handles reservations and answers common questions
- Multilingual support serves international travelers
- Voice-enabled for hands-free interaction
- Event inquiry collection and qualification
- Integration with booking and reservation systems

## Financial Services

**Pain points:**
- High volume of account and product inquiries
- Compliance requirements for customer interactions
- Customers expecting instant responses for time-sensitive matters
- Complex product explanations needed across channels

**How Aivonic helps:**
- AI agent handles routine account inquiries and product information
- Strict guardrails and compliance-aware responses
- Escalation to licensed representatives for regulated advice
- Infinite memory maintains relationship context across interactions
- Secure handling of sensitive information through the Crust security gateway
