# Aivonic - Voice and Language Support

## Voice Conversations

Customers can speak to your agent instead of typing. They click the microphone button in the chat widget, speak naturally, and the message sends automatically when they stop speaking. The agent processes the spoken message and can respond with natural-sounding voice output.

Voice responses only trigger when the customer uses voice input. If they type, they get text-only responses. This keeps the experience natural and avoids unexpected audio.

## Voice Providers

Three options depending on your needs and budget:

ElevenLabs provides high-quality, human-like voices with excellent multilingual support across 29 languages. This is the most popular choice for businesses serving international customers.

Hume AI offers emotionally intelligent voices that adapt their tone to the context of the conversation. Their voices sound warm and natural with subtle emotional expression. This is the premium choice for businesses where customer experience is critical.

OpenAI TTS provides reliable, consistent voice quality using your existing OpenAI API key. Six voice options are available. Good for getting started without additional API costs.

## How to Set Up Voice

Voice is optional and can be enabled from your client portal dashboard under Settings. You select your preferred provider, enter the API key, and choose a voice. A preview button lets you hear how each voice sounds before saving. Changes take effect immediately.

## Language Support

Agents automatically detect and respond in the customer's language. No configuration required. If a customer writes in Swedish, the agent responds in Swedish. If they switch to English mid-conversation, the agent switches too.

Supported languages include English, Swedish, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Polish, Russian, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Arabic, Hindi, Turkish, Czech, Danish, Finnish, Greek, Hungarian, Indonesian, Malay, Norwegian, Romanian, Slovak, Thai, Ukrainian, and Vietnamese.

All features work across all languages. Voice output, knowledge base search, skills, and memory all function regardless of the language being used.

## Voice Input

Voice input uses the browser's built-in speech recognition (Web Speech API). It works in Chrome, Edge, and most modern browsers. No additional software or plugins needed. The customer clicks the microphone button, speaks, and the speech is converted to text automatically.

## Common Questions About Voice

Can I hear the voice before choosing? Yes. The Voice Picker in your dashboard includes a Preview button next to each voice option.

Does voice cost extra? The voice feature itself is included. You pay for usage through your chosen voice provider's API pricing (ElevenLabs, Hume AI, or OpenAI).

Do customers need to install anything? No. Voice works directly in the browser. No downloads, plugins, or apps needed.

What if a customer prefers typing? Voice is always optional. The text input is always available. Customers choose whichever they prefer.
