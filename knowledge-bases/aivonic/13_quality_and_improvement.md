# Aivonic - Quality Assurance and Self-Improvement

## Pre-Deployment Evaluation

No agent goes live without passing a mandatory 4-phase quality evaluation. This is not optional.

Phase 1 - Infrastructure: all systems are verified healthy, API connections confirmed, and technical dependencies tested.

Phase 2 - Functional: automated test cases cover greeting behavior, skill execution, edge cases, error handling, and guardrails. The agent must pass 90% or more of functional tests.

Phase 3 - Quality: an AI evaluator scores the agent's responses on relevance, accuracy, and helpfulness across a range of realistic scenarios. The agent must score 7.0 out of 10 or higher.

Phase 4 - Security: the agent is tested for prompt injection resistance and jailbreak protection to ensure it stays within its defined boundaries.

If issues are detected, the system automatically fixes them and re-tests up to 3 times. Most problems resolve without human intervention.

## Certification Badges

Based on evaluation scores, each agent receives a visible certification level in your portal:

Standard: quality score 7.0+ out of 10, functional rate 90%+. Your agent meets professional standards and is ready for production.

Premium: quality score 8.0+ out of 10, functional rate 95%+. Your agent delivers consistently excellent responses.

Elite: quality score 8.5+ out of 10, functional rate 100%. Your agent performs at the highest level across all test scenarios.

## Continuous Quality Monitoring

After launch, every conversation is automatically scored. The system tracks quality trends over time and flags any issues immediately. You can see quality scores and breakdowns in your analytics dashboard.

## Self-Improvement

Agents learn from their conversations automatically. The system identifies patterns in successful interactions and applies improvements without manual intervention. Common questions get better answers over time. The agent's understanding of your business deepens with every conversation.

## Earned Autonomy

Aivonic agents can earn greater operational independence through demonstrated performance. This is governed by four levels:

Supervised: every output is available for review. This is where new agents start.

Reviewed: the agent operates with periodic batch reviews of its outputs.

Monitored: the agent operates independently with exception-based review only.

Autonomous: full operational independence with periodic audits.

Progression through these levels is based on measurable trust scores calculated from the agent's track record. An agent earns autonomy through consistent, high-quality performance over time. Clients can set maximum autonomy levels and require review for specific output types through the portal dashboard.

## What This Means For You

Your agent starts strong (thanks to pre-deployment evaluation) and gets better over time (thanks to self-improvement and earned autonomy). Quality is not a one-time check but a continuous process. No other platform in this price range offers this level of quality assurance.
