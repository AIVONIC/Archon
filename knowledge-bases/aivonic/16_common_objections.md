# Aivonic - Common Questions and Concerns

## "What if the AI gives wrong information?"

Multiple safeguards prevent this. The agent is trained only on accurate information you provide. It is designed to say "I don't know" rather than guess. Every conversation is automatically scored for quality. Issues are caught and fixed quickly. For anything truly sensitive, the agent escalates to your team rather than risk being wrong.

## "Will customers know they're talking to AI?"

We recommend transparency because customers appreciate honesty. The conversation feels natural, not robotic. Many customers do not particularly care whether they are talking to AI or a human, as long as they get help quickly and accurately.

## "What about our existing customer service team?"

The AI handles the repetitive work: routine questions, after-hours inquiries, simple requests, and initial qualification. Your team handles the high-value work: complex issues, sensitive matters, relationship building, and decisions requiring human judgment. The result is your team focuses on what they do best while routine matters are handled instantly.

## "We tried chatbots before and they were terrible."

Traditional chatbots force customers through rigid menu trees with pre-written responses. Aivonic agents are fundamentally different. They understand natural language, maintain context across conversations, remember returning customers, take real actions (email, scheduling, payments), and improve over time. They are closer to a capable team member than a menu system.

## "Is it secure?"

Yes. SSL encryption for all data in transit. Secure enterprise-grade hosting. No data sharing with third parties. Security gateway with Data Loss Prevention and prompt injection protection. Every agent is security-tested before deployment. You own all conversation data and can delete it anytime.

## "What if my business changes?"

We update your agent as your business evolves. Changes to business hours, new products, updated policies, or new services can all be reflected quickly. Minor updates are included in your support plan. Larger changes like adding new service areas or restructuring how the agent handles conversations may require a separate quote.

## "It seems expensive."

Consider the alternative. A human handling the same workload would cost significantly more in salary, benefits, training, and management. The agent works 24/7 without breaks, sick days, or turnover. Most clients see positive ROI within the first month through reduced support costs, captured after-hours leads, and faster response times.

For comparison, DIY chatbot platforms cost $50-$500 per month but require days of setup, offer limited capabilities, and still need your team to manage them. Custom AI development costs $100K-$500K+ and takes months. Aivonic delivers enterprise-quality agents at a fraction of the cost with zero technical effort from you.

## "Do I need to provide an API key?"

Yes. You provide your own API key, which means you have full control over your AI costs and can monitor usage directly. We never see or store your conversations on our side beyond what is needed for the agent to function.

## "How is this different from ChatGPT?"

ChatGPT is a general-purpose AI assistant. An Aivonic agent is a custom-built specialist trained on your business, embedded on your website, capable of taking real actions (booking appointments, sending emails, processing payments), and designed to convert visitors into customers. It knows your products, follows your brand voice, and works within guardrails you define.

## "What happens when you go out of business?"

Your conversations and knowledge base data are exportable at any time. The chat widget can be removed with a single line of code. There is no vendor lock-in. Your API key is yours.

## "Can it handle multiple languages?"

Yes, automatically. The agent detects the customer's language on every message and responds in the same language. 29 languages supported. No configuration required. If a customer switches languages mid-conversation, the agent adapts immediately.

## "What about GDPR?"

You own all conversation data. Customers can request data deletion and you can comply immediately through the portal. We do not share data with third parties. Data is stored on secure servers.
