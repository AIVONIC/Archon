# Aivonic - Company Overview

## Who We Are

Aivonic AB is a Swedish AI agent development company. We build, deploy, and manage custom AI agents for businesses. We are not a DIY chatbot platform - we build it for you.

Founded by Christian, Aivonic is a full-stack AI agent platform where every agent is custom-built to fit the client's business, industry, and workflows. The total time investment from a client is roughly one hour for a discovery call and onboarding form. We handle everything else.

## What We Do

We create intelligent AI agents that live on client websites as chat widgets, handling customer conversations, automating workflows, and integrating with existing business tools. Every agent is tailored to the client's specific needs - from the personality and tone of voice to the skills and integrations it uses.

Our agents are not generic chatbots. They are purpose-built conversational AI systems with:

- Multilingual support with automatic language detection
- Voice-enabled conversations (text and speech)
- Infinite memory that remembers every customer interaction
- Pre-deployment quality certification
- Enterprise-grade security with prompt injection protection
- Deep integration capabilities (email, CRM, scheduling, payments, and more)

## Mission

To make enterprise-grade AI agents accessible to businesses of all sizes, without requiring technical expertise or months of development time. We handle the complexity so our clients can focus on their business.

## How It Works

1. **Discovery call** - We learn about the client's business, goals, and requirements
2. **We build the agent** - Custom-configured with the right skills, personality, and integrations
3. **Quality certification** - Every agent passes a 4-phase evaluation before deployment
4. **Deployment** - Chat widget installed on the client's website, ready to serve customers
5. **Ongoing management** - We handle updates, monitoring, and optimization
