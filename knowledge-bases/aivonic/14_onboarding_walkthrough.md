# Aivonic - Getting Started

## The Five-Step Process

### Step 1: Discovery Call (15-30 minutes)

A brief conversation where we understand your business, identify your most common customer questions, and select the right agent configuration. No preparation needed. Just tell us about your business, what problems you want the agent to solve, and what tone fits your brand.

Book at aivonic.ai/discovery or email christian@aivonic.ai.

### Step 2: Receive Your Agent ID

After the call, our systems automatically create an agent template based on your requirements and email you a unique Agent ID with a link to your onboarding form.

### Step 3: Complete the Onboarding Form (about 10 minutes)

A simple online form where you provide your company name and industry, your website URL, agent customization (name, greeting, tone, brand colors), your API key, and optionally upload documents or enter website URLs for the knowledge base.

### Step 4: Automatic Deployment (5-15 minutes)

Once you submit the form, everything happens automatically. Your agent is configured with your business information, your knowledge base is processed, infrastructure is provisioned, quality evaluation runs, and the agent deploys to production. No human intervention required.

### Step 5: You Are Live

You receive an email with login credentials for your Client Portal and embed code for your website. Paste one line of code before the closing body tag on your site. Your agent appears and starts helping customers immediately.

Total time from discovery call to live agent: approximately 1 hour. Your total time investment: about 30 minutes.

## What You Need to Provide

At minimum: company name, contact email, agent customization preferences, and an API key.

Optionally: documents (FAQs, product catalogs, policies), website URLs to crawl, sample conversations if available, and any specific instructions for how the agent should handle certain topics.

## Do I Need Technical Skills?

None at all. Adding the widget to your site means pasting one line of code, similar to adding Google Analytics. If even that feels daunting, we can do it for you.

The widget works on WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Squarespace, and any platform that supports JavaScript.

## After Launch

Your agent is monitored 24/7 with automatic quality scoring. You receive weekly performance summaries by email and monthly detailed analytics. The agent improves continuously based on real conversations.

Support is available per your plan. Standard support provides email with 24-hour response time. Premium support provides priority response within 4 hours plus a dedicated Slack channel.

## What Success Looks Like

First week: the widget is live, customers are getting instant answers, you can see conversations in your dashboard.

First month: response patterns are identified, the agent improves based on real data, your first performance report arrives.

Third month: the agent handles the majority of routine inquiries, staff time is freed up, customer satisfaction is measurable.

Ongoing: the agent continues improving automatically, costs stay predictable, you focus on growing your business.

## Common Questions

How long does setup take? Under 1 hour total. The deployment is fully automated.

What if I am not satisfied? 30-day money-back guarantee on your setup fee. No questions asked.

Can I make changes after launch? Yes. You can update your agent's settings, knowledge base, and configuration through the portal at any time. Monthly revisions are included in your support plan.

What happens if the AI cannot answer something? It acknowledges the customer's question, collects their contact information, and notifies your team. This is far better than leaving customers without any response.
