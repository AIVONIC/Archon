# Algarve ForSale - Algarve Real Estate Market (2025-2026)

## Market Overview

The Algarve continues to be one of Portugal's strongest property markets. Key data points:

- The Algarve recorded approximately 13.8-16.6% year-on-year price growth in 2025
- Average property price in the Algarve: approximately EUR 3,200-3,467 per square metre
- Portugal led the EU in property price growth in 2025
- Market forecast for 2026: moderate continued growth of 2-4%, shifting from rapid appreciation to more stable, fundamentals-driven growth
- Supply remains constrained, particularly in the western Algarve where buildable land is limited

## International Buyer Demographics

- British buyers remain the dominant foreign nationality in the Algarve
- German buyers represent the second or third largest foreign group
- American buyers have surged from under 1% pre-pandemic to approximately 10% of foreign purchases nationally, with higher concentrations in premium Algarve segments
- This growth is partly driven by new direct flights (United Airlines Newark-Faro route)
- Scandinavian, French, and Dutch buyers also active

## Western Algarve Specifically

The western Algarve (Lagos to Sagres, including Burgau) is characterised by:
- More rugged, unspoilt coastline protected by Costa Vicentina natural park
- Lower density development compared to central Algarve (Albufeira, Vilamoura)
- Growing premium positioning -- attracting quality over volume
- Limited new development due to planning restrictions
- Stronger appeal to lifestyle buyers versus pure holiday tourists
- Increasingly popular with remote workers and digital nomads

## New Hospitality Permits

Obtaining new hospitality/tourism permits in the western Algarve is becoming increasingly difficult. Planning restrictions in the Costa Vicentina protected area limit new construction. This makes existing permitted structures (like this property) significantly more valuable -- the buyer inherits not just the building but the entitlement.
