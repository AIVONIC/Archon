# Algarve ForSale - Practical Information

## Getting to Burgau

- Nearest airport: Faro (FAO), approximately 1 hour drive
- Direct flights from: London, Manchester, Dublin, Frankfurt, Munich, Berlin, Amsterdam, Paris, Stockholm, and seasonal routes from many European cities
- United Airlines operates direct Newark-Faro flights (significant for American buyers)
- Car rental available at Faro airport; recommended for exploring the western Algarve
- Lagos has a train station connecting to Faro and Lisbon via the regional rail network
- Transfer services available from Faro airport

## Language

Portuguese is the official language. English is widely spoken in the Algarve, particularly in tourist areas and among real estate professionals. German and French are also commonly understood. The AI agent on this site can communicate in English, Portuguese, and German.

## Currency

Portugal uses the Euro.

## Safety

Portugal is consistently ranked among the safest countries in the world. The 2024 Global Peace Index ranked Portugal 7th globally. The Algarve is particularly known for its low crime rates and welcoming community.
